Post Covid-19 Real Estate Trends

  1. Home
  2. Blog

With more and more people realising that home is the new office and also the new entertainment zone due to the lockdown experience, they are looking to upsize their homes and looking to invest in services that bring a quality of life without compromising on the luxury quotient. Starting with bespoke experiences to multi-functional spaces and amenities, and safety systems, everyone is now looking for stable, comfortable and sustainable real estate assets during these volatile times.

With the rise of the work-from-home culture, there has been a robust demand in high-priced properties that can serve as work-life spaces and also offer a resort-like living experience with all modern amenities. The real estate market in India has therefore evolved fast in the past 1 year with demands for ready-to-move-in houses and villas that offer a wholesome lifestyle along with elegant architecture that support both modern and functional visions of buyers. Housing affordability has increased with the positive impact of the inoculation program. Even though the stress caused by the Coronavirus pandemic, has impacted India’s key residential markets, the average rates of new projects continue to stick to their previous levels, data available with PropTiger.com show a 2% rise in West Bengal. No prime markets have shown any downward movement in real estate trends in spite of the severe pressure caused on growth.

Home buyers are now looking for exclusivity with a touch of personalisation to have a space where work and personal life can peacefully, if not luxuriously, coexist. They value the authenticity of thoughtful designs and technology to create a space that is functional, bespoke and well-connected which will become an ecosystem of choices to live, work and play simultaneously.

One size doesn’t fit all, and that’s why Ananta Residency offers Simplex and Duplex villas as well as premium plots for personalised buildups for all, newly wed first time home buyers, retired couples, couples planning to start a family for better space options, and for those too who are looking for one lifetime asset that can become a family home.

You can’t really put a price tag on emotions and people today are willing to spend extra when it comes to building spaces for life and family. Everyone looks for both exclusivity and individuality, a space they can call home, a corner which can serve as a home-office. Ananta Residency thus stands out from the crowd with offers of personalisation of customers’ individual experience and their relationship with the brand.

“Love thy neighbour” - Home buyers also look for the feeling of community, a sense of belonging with the neighbours within the same ecosystem. At Ananta Residency, the houses are planned in such a way that gives you privacy but also keeps you close enough from your neighbours to reach out to each other at times of need and at times where you feel like mingling. The need to collaborate and engage with like-minded people is the foremost thought behind building the Ananta Residency community.

Buyers are more conscious now, they care for the environment and prefer eco-friendly options for every little detail in their lives. Ananta Residency is by far the largest project in Asansol which has 25 gardens to give you the fresh green feeling when you are at home. We are doing our bit to contribute to the improvement of the environment and building gardens with trees that help in curbing pollution, attract birds and garden animals to create a green oasis in an urban set-up. The boom in sustainable buildings is undoubtedly driving new levels of innovation in architecture and aesthetics.
